Lexis Hibiscus

Lexis Hibiscus Port Dickson is a luxury resort located in Port Dickson, Malaysia. It is known for its unique design featuring a cluster of luxury villas shaped like hibiscus flowers floating on the sea.

The resort offers over 600 spacious villas, each equipped with a private dip pool and steam room. Guests can enjoy a range of facilities including a spa, water sports, and several dining options.

Lexis Hibiscus is a popular destination for both local and international tourists looking for a luxurious and relaxing getaway by the sea.
